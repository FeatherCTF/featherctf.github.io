What's The Feather?
It's a Capture the Flag event held under CyQuest event organised by Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics division of Vellore Institute of Technology,Bhopal

[CyQuest 2.0]

Overnight Madness

fCTF is overnight event. Only the toughest of all stand a chance, CTF starts at 12:00 am [27-03-20] (after 9hr-long Day 1 CyQuest hackathon) and ends at 6:00 am [27-03-20] (3hrs before day 2 hackathon session)

The one who manage to earn points in CTF, raises winning prabability of his/her team at hackathon as points are merged !

Framework

Feather CTF (fCTF) framework is build on python3, flask, basic HTML,just enough CSS and simple JS with SQLite3 Database to manage Scores and Flags. The only thing that's simple in our CTF is it's Framework, and we will OpenSource the fCTF Framework after Feather CTF 0x1
